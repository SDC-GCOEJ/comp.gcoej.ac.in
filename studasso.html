Student Association

COMPACT

The Department has a students association named as COMPACT.COMPACT, the Computer association for creative technocrats of GCOE Jalgaon , aims to play a pivotal role in the development of students as engineers by various out of curriculum and extracurricular activities. COMPACT aims to inculcate among its members an awareness and appreciation of the various disciplines. By way of its activities COMPACT aims to be a platform for all the students of GCOE Jalgaon, in general and particularly of the students of Computer Engineering Department. Every year the COMPACT day is celebrated by organizing the poster competition, technical quiz, paper presentation, Rangoli competition, parents meet etc. The other activities include Wall Magazine, expert lectures, Computer hardware workshop, etc.
